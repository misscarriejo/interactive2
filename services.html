Our Services

Basic Eye Examination

Includes a fully comprehensive examination of the eye to check vision and to screen for cataracts, macular degeneration, glaucoma, and other medical conditions. Also included is a written prescription for glasses, if needed.

A Fundus examination, which is used to evaluate the health of the eye, is also part of the full examination. This diagnostic procedure can be conducted by using either dilation drops or Optomap (digital retinal scan).

If the patient decides to go with the dilation drops we recommend the patient brings someone to drive them afterwards because they will be experiencing side effects of blurry vision and ultra sensitivity to light. The side effects usually last on average 2 - 3 hours. Dilation drops are included in the initial price.

Alternatively the Optomap is a noninvasive retinal scan of the back of the eye. This method has no post side effects that are associated with dilation drops. Optomap is an additional $35 charge.

Contact Lens Examination

If the patient decides to go with the dilation drops we recommend the patient bring someone to drive them afterwards because they will be experiencing side effects of blurry vision and ultra sensitivity to light. The side effects usually last on average 2 - 3 hours. Dilation drops are included in the initial price.

Alternatively the Optomap is a noninvasive retinal scan of the back of the eye. This method has no post side effects that are associated with dilation drops. Optomap is an additional $35 charge.

For up to 3 months contact lens checks are complementary.

For first time contact lens wearers a $10 contact lens training session must be completed. However it can be rescheduled for a later date and time.

Contact Lens Fitting

Includes a written prescription for contact lenses.

For first time contact lens wearers, a $10 contact lens training session must be completed. However, it can be rescheduled for a later date and time.

Patient must have a recent written prescription, within three months of scheduled fitting.

Office Visit

In addition to your annual eye examination, as optometrists, our doctors are licensed to treat and prescribe for a number of eye injuries and ailments. Examples of office visits are foreign objects in the eye, pink eye, eye pain, etc.

If you are experiencing severe eye pain and need to be seen for immediate care, please go to the Emergency Room.
