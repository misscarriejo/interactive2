Insurance Policy

Insurance

Presently, Dr. Ambo & Associates only directly bills insurance for individuals covered under an Aetna Costco employee insurance plan. If you are covered by Aetna Costco employee insurance, please make sure to provide us with your Aetna ID number.

However, for all other insurance companies, we will be happy to provide you with an itemized receipt which you can use to submit a claim with your insurance company, provided that your policy covers eye examinations.

Medicare

  • Medicare claim reimbursements are not accepted at this office.
  • We may bill your Medicare claim for you. We need a copy of your Medicare Card, personal information, and your signature for submission.
  • When your Supplemental Insurance is Primary, you can submit a claim and it will be forwarded to Medicare.
  • Medicare does not consider a routine eye exam to be a medical necessity under certain diagnosis. These procedures are denied by Medicare.
  • This office is submitting claims electronically over the internet. Your personal information will be kept confidential according to HIPAA policy.

If you have any questions regarding our Medicare or insurance policy, please don't hesitate to ask one of our knowledgeable technicians when you visit. Or, give us a call at (425) 369-6726.
