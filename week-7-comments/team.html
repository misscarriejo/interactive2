Our Approach

Dr. Ambo and Associates has been established since 1989 and has since been providing its patients with continued vision care services. Our success is owed in part to our highly knowledgeable doctors and experienced technicians who go out of their way to help accommodate patients. The other part of our success is due to the many long term relationships we have established with patients over the years.

We pride ourselves in our ability to offer an affordable, friendly experience utilizing the most advanced technology, equipment and procedures. This ensures our patients a level of unprecedented excellence in their vision care experience.
